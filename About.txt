Operations Overview
Text to Speech:
The 'Text to Speech' tab allows you to convert entered text into speech. Choose a language and adjust the speed to convert text into audio files.

Text to PDF:
In the 'Text to PDF' tab, you can convert entered text into a downloadable PDF file.

Word to PDF:
The 'Word to PDF' tab enables you to upload a Word document and convert it to a PDF file.

Requirements
Python 3.7+
Streamlit
gTTS (Google Text-to-Speech)
FPDF
docx2pdf
